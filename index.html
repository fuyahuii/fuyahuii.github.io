Yahui Fu

Yoshidahonmachi, Sakyo Ward, Kyoto, Japan

I am a program-specific researcher at Kyoto University in Kyoto, Japan. I obtained my Ph.D. in Intelligence Science and Technology from Kyoto University under the supervision of Prof. Tatsuya Kawahara. My research focuses on Dialogue Systems, including empathetic dialogue generation, personality-aware conversational models, emotion recognition, and human-agent/robot interaction. I am also interested in explainable and controllable LLMs, particularly in causal reasoning and user-adaptive methods.

I'm currently on the job market and open to opportunities across academia and industry. If our interests align, let's connect.
